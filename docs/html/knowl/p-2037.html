Let's check with the test vector \(p(x)=1+x+x^2\text{.}\) We have \((p)_B=(1,1,1)\text{.}\) Thus we should have \([p]_{B'}=\underset{B\rightarrow B'}{P}[p]_B=\begin{bmatrix}1\amp 2\amp 4\\ 0\amp 1\amp 4\\ 0\amp 0\amp 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} =\begin{bmatrix}7\\ 5\\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\text{.}\) Equivalently, this means that \(p(x)=7+5(x-2)+(x-2)^2\text{,}\) as one easily verifies.
