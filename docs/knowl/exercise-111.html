  1. Let \(W=\{(x,y)\in \R^2\colon 2x+3y=0\}\text{.}\)

  2. Let \(W=\{(x,y)\in \R^2\colon \val{x}\geq \val{y}\}\text{.}\)

  3. Let \(W=\{(x,y)\in \R^2\colon x^2+2y^2\leq 1\}\text{.}\)
