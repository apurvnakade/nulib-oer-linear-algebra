Step 2

Let \(\bolda_j\) be the \(j\)-th column of \(A\text{,}\) considered as a column vector. Given any column vector \(\boldb=[b_{i}]_{n\times 1}\) we have

\begin{equation*} A\,\boldb=A\,\begin{bmatrix}b_1\\b_2\\ \vdots \\b_n \end{bmatrix} = b_1\bolda_1+b_2\bolda_2+\cdots +b_n\bolda_n\text{.} \end{equation*}
