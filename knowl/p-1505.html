To determine whether a function of sets \(f\colon X\rightarrow Y\) is injective, we normally have to show that for each output \(y\) in the image of \(f\) there is exactly one input \(x\) satisfying \(f(x)=y\text{.}\) Think of this as checking injectivity at every output.
