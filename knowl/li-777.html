Contracting to a basis

Let \(W=\Span S\text{.}\) To contract \(S\) to a basis \(W\text{,}\) proceed as follows.

  1. Pick any ordered basis \(B\) of \(V\) and let \(S'=\{[\boldv_1]_B, [\boldv_2]_B, \dots, [\boldv_r]_B\}\subseteq \R^n\text{.}\)

  2. Use the relevant procedure of ProcedureÂ 3.8.13 to contract \(S'\) to a basis \(T'=\{[\boldv_{i_1}]_B, [\boldv_{i_2}]_B, \dots, [\boldv_{i_k}]_B\}\) of \(\Span S'\text{.}\)

  3. The set \(T=\{\boldv_{i_1}, \boldv_{i_2}, \dots, \boldv_{i_k}\}\subseteq S\) is a basis for \(W=\Span S\text{.}\)
