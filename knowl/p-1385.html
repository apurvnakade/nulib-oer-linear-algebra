Let \(X\subseteq \R\) be an interval. The sets \(C(X), C^n(X), C^\infty(X), P_n(X), P(X)\) are all subspaces of \(F(X,\R)\text{.}\) Thus we have the following chain of subspaces:

\begin{equation*} P_n(X)\subseteq P(X)\subseteq C^\infty(X)\subseteq C^n(X)\subseteq C(X)\subseteq F(X,\R)\text{.} \end{equation*}
