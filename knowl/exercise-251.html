Prove: columns \(\boldb_{i_1}, \boldb_{i_2}, \dots, \boldb_{i_r}\) of \(B\) are linearly independent if and only if the corresponding columns \(\bolda_{i_1}, \bolda_{i_2},\dots, \bolda_{i_r}\) are linearly independent.
