As the title of Theorem 4.2.9 suggests, there is a follow-up result (Corollary 4.7.4), and this states that in fact any linear transformation \(T\colon\R^n\rightarrow\R^m \) is of the form \(T=T_A\) for some \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\text{.}\) In other words, all linear transformations from \(\R^n\) to \(\R^m\) are matrix transformations.
