  1. \(A\in M_{33}\text{,}\) \(p(t)=\det(tI-A)=t^3-t^2\text{,}\) \(\nullity A=2\)

  2. \(A\in M_{33}\text{,}\) \(p(t)=\det(tI-A)=t^3+t^2-t\)

  3. \(A\in M_{22}\text{,}\) \(\tr A=4\text{,}\) \(\det A=3\)
