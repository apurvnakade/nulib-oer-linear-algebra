Theorem 5.3.20. Change of basis for transformations.

Let \(V\) be finite-dimensional, let \(T\colon V\rightarrow V\) be linear, and let \(B\) and \(B'\) be two ordered bases for \(V\text{.}\) We have

\begin{equation} [T]_{B'}=\underset{B\rightarrow B'}{P}\, [T]_B\, \underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P}\text{,}\tag{5.3.5} \end{equation}

or equivalently

\begin{equation} [T]_{B'}=\underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P}^{-1}\, [T]_B\, \underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P}\text{.}\tag{5.3.6} \end{equation}

Proof.
