A plane in \(\R^3\) is the graph \(\mathcal{P}\) of a linear equation of the form \(ax+by+cz=d\text{,}\) where \(a, b, c, d\in\R\) are fixed constants, and at least one of \(a, b, c\) is nonzero: i.e.,

\begin{equation*} \mathcal{P}=\{(x,y,z)\in\R^3\colon ax+by+cz=d\}. \end{equation*}
