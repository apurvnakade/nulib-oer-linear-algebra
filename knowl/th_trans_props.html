Theorem 2.2.11. Properties of matrix transposition.

The following properties hold for all matrices \(A, B, C\) and scalars \(c\in \R\) for which the given expression makes sense.

  1. \(\displaystyle (A+B)^T=A^T+B^T\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (cA)^T=cA^T\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (AB)^T=B^TA^T\)

  4. \(\displaystyle \left(A^T\right)^T=A\)
