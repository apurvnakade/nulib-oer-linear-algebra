Exercise 1.

Verify that \(\R^\infty=\{(a_i)_{i=1}^\infty\colon a_i\in \R\}\text{,}\) along with the proposed vector operations

\begin{align*} c(a_i)_{i=1}^\infty\amp=(ca_i)_{i=1}^\infty \\ (a_i)_{i=1}^\infty+(b_i)_{i=1}^\infty \amp =(a_i+b_i)_{i=1}^\infty \text{,} \end{align*}

satisfies the defining axioms of a vector space.
