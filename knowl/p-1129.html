By definition of scalar multiplication in \(\R_{>0}\) (Definition 3.1.10) we have

\begin{equation*} 3\boldv=2^3=8 \text{ and }(-1/5)\boldw=(1/2)^{-1/5}=\sqrt[5]{2}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Next, since vector addition in \(\R_{>0}\) is defined as real number multiplication (Definition 3.1.10), we conclude

\begin{equation*} 3\boldv+(-1/5)\boldw=8\sqrt[5]{2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
