Item 2.

Recall that the zero vector of \(P_2\) is the zero polynomial \(\boldzero=0x^2+0x+0\text{.}\) We have

\begin{align*} a(x^2+x-2)+b(2x^2+1)+c(x^2-x)=\boldzero \amp \iff (a+2b+c)x^2+(a-c)x+(-2a+b)=0x^2+0x+0 \\ \amp \\ \amp \iff \begin{linsys}{3} a\amp +\amp 2b\amp +\amp c\amp =\amp 0\\ a\amp \amp \amp -\amp c\amp =\amp 0\\ -2a\amp +\amp b \amp \amp \amp =\amp 0 \end{linsys} \amp (\knowl{./knowl/rm_polynomial_equality.html}{\text{Remark 3.3.22}})\text{.} \end{align*}

Gaussian elimination shows that \((a,b,c)=(0,0,0)\) is the unique solution to this last system. We conclude that \(S\) is linearly independent.
